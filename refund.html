Refund Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

1. Digital Nature & Refund Exclusions

Due to the intangible nature of downloadable digital files, all sales are considered final and non-refundable once the direct file link has been dispatched on your checkout success page.

If you encounter technical file issues, download link expirations, or file corruption during extraction, please reach out to our team via Contact Support. We will dispatch replacement files to your billing email within 12 hours.

In cases of confirmed accidental duplicate transactions (purchasing the same digital product twice within a 24-hour window), we will gladly approve a full refund of the duplicate charge. Simply contact us with your Payhip transaction tokens.
